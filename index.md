# Description
My name is Samuel Louviot, I come from France, I live in New York City, and I am a Neural Engineer.

For those who don't really know what "Neural Engineering" means: I discovered this term when I was working at the City College of New York under the supervision of [Pr. Jacek Dmochowski](https://dmochow.github.io/about/) and I thought this term describes quite well my profile and what I want to become.
- **Neural:** I hold a PhD in Health and Life Science with a concentration in Human Neuroscience.
- **Engineer:** I have an associate degree in electrical engineering, and B.Sc and M.Sc in Biomedical Engineering (with a concentration in Cybernetics/Robotics, instrumentation, and image for biology and medicine).

I am passionate about science and engineering, learning, coding, soldering, playing around with my 3 RaspberryPi and I love nature and try my best to orient all my actions toward the protection of our planet. 

This blog is to put down somewhere, like a bottle in the sea (metaphorically. Don't put bottles in the sea please), my humble thought and my perspective on some topic such as neural engineering (but not only). With the hope that one day this blog will help somebody.

Enjoy!
